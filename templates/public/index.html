A better day starts with people

Better at Shea means better together.

At Shea Post Acute Rehabilitation Center, residents come to recover strength, mobility, confidence, and hope. The work is clinical, but the feeling is deeply human: one steady hand, one clean room, one therapy win, one meal served with care.

We want people who notice the little things and take pride in making hard days softer. Around here, better days get built by teams who show up for residents and for each other.
