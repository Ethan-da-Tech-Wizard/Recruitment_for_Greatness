About Shea PARC

Local care, real growth, and a team built around people. Around here, we call it the Shea Way.

What Shea PARC Is

Shea Post Acute Rehabilitation Center, known locally as Shea PARC, is a 120-bed Scottsdale skilled nursing and rehabilitation center focused on helping guests regain strength, mobility, confidence, and independence after illness, surgery, or injury.

Our team works closely with residents, families, physicians, therapists, nurses, and support departments to create care plans that are personal, practical, and centered on each resident's goals.

That same team-first mindset shapes the employee experience. Whether someone joins nursing, therapy, culinary, activities, social services, EVS, maintenance, or administration, the work is connected by the same purpose: making Shea PARC feel welcoming, capable, and steady every day. It is care with a little extra sunshine, the Shea Way.

How We Help Residents Get Better

Skilled Nursing Care

24-hour nursing care for residents requiring ongoing medical attention and support with daily activities.

Short-Term Rehabilitation

Long-Term Care

Compassionate care for residents who need ongoing assistance and medical supervision.

Memory Care

Specialized programs for residents with Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia.
