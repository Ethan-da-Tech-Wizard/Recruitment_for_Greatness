About Shea PARC

Local care, hands-on leadership, and a team built around people. Around here, we call it the Shea Way.

What Shea PARC Is

Shea Post Acute Rehabilitation Center, known locally as Shea PARC, is a 120-bed Scottsdale skilled nursing and rehabilitation center focused on helping guests regain strength, mobility, confidence, and independence after illness, surgery, or injury.

Care here is personal and practical. Residents, families, physicians, therapists, nurses, and support teams work around goals that matter in real life.

That same team-first mindset shapes the workday. Nursing, therapy, culinary, activities, social services, EVS, maintenance, and admin all carry the same purpose: make Shea feel capable, kind, and steady every day.
